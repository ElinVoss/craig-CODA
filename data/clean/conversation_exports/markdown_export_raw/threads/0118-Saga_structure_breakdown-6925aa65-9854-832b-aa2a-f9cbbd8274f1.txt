## 3. STRUCTURAL LAWS

### Novella Format (MANDATORY)

Every novella follows this exact structure:

**Components:**
- **15 numbered chapters** (main story, Jhace/Tiffani POV)
- **2 unnumbered interludes** (Auditor-narrated god POVs, 2 gods per interlude)
- **1 unnumbered epilogue** (Auditor's observations)

**Word Count Law:**
- Every chapter: **800–1300 words** (NEVER exceed 1300)
- Every interlude: **800–1300 words** (split between 2 god POVs)
- Every epilogue: **800–1300 words**

**Total per novella:** ~14,400–23,400 words

### Interlude Placement Rules (MANDATORY)

1. Interludes fall **between chapters 4 and 13** only
2. **Never less than 2 chapters** between interludes
3. **Placement must vary** per novella (never repeat same pattern)
4. Each interlude contains **two god POVs** separated by page break

**Example valid placements:**
- N01: After Ch 5, After Ch 11
- N02: After Ch 7, After Ch 12
- N03: After Ch 4, After Ch 9

# 50 – LEXICON & STYLE GUIDE
**Prose Rules and Measurement Usage for Confluence Chronicles**

---

## 1. DOCUMENT PURPOSE

This guide defines how the story is written on the page: narrative voice, formatting, language constraints, and how the Te'Oga measurement systems are used in prose.  
It exists to keep all 30 novellas consistent in tone, clarity, and in-world terminology.

---

## 2. PROSE LAWS (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### 2.1 Point of View

- **Main chapters:** Close third person, anchored in the current POV character (usually Jhace or Tiffani in Turning 1, Jhace-focused later).  
- Camera stays inside their sensory and conceptual limits—no omniscient commentary in main chapters.  
- Interludes and epilogues are **Auditor-narrated** (see Section 6).

### 2.2 Tense

- Default: **tight past tense** for main chapters.  
- Short present-tense flashes allowed only for immediate, high-stress sensations (pain, impact, realization), and must be brief.

### 2.3 Language Constraints

- **No metaphors or similes** in main chapters.  
  - Do not write "like" / "as if" comparisons for imagery.  
  - Describe what is literally present: light, sound, motion, weight, temperature, stress.  
- Descriptions must be **plain, concrete, and physical**.  
  - "The beam bowed two fingers under the load" is correct.  
  - "The beam sagged like an old man" is not.

### 2.4 Magic Description (Grounded and Anime-Escalated)

- Soulpulse use is always described through **material effects and bodily cost**, not abstract energy:
  - Show beams straightening, glass knitting, joints locking, stone crumbling.  
  - Show shaking hands, shortness of breath, pain, exhaustion.  
- Even at late-saga anime levels:
  - No color-coded auras (no "blue aura of Purity," etc.).  
  - No vague "energy blasts" without specifying pressure, shrapnel, heat, concussion, or structural result.  
  - If someone "flies," it must be grounded (platforms of locked debris, wind pressure control, etc.).

### 2.5 Cost Signaling

Every on-page use of Soulpulse must show **some** cost, scaled to intensity:

- Light use: tremor, tension, extra breathing.  
- Heavy use: shaking limbs, partial collapse, muddled thoughts.  
- Fathom Break / god-tier: near-total collapse, lasting injury.

There is no cost-free use of Soulpulse. If it looks effortless, the pain is deferred, not erased.

---

## 3. MEASUREMENT LEXICON USAGE

All in-world measurement uses the Te'Oga systems: time, distance, and mass.  
Metric/imperial terms (meters, miles, kilograms, seconds, weeks) **never** appear in character-facing prose.

## 2. World & Cosmology: Te’Oga, the Piercing, and Why Magic Exists at All

### 2.1 The Piercing (01-TeOga-Piercing)

- A **~200 km spindle-shaped projectile** hits Te’Oga:
  - Entry: northwest ocean.  
  - Exit: at the “Crown” – the key city where Jhace/Tiffani live.
  - Bores straight through crust → mantle → near-core → back out.

- Consequences:
  - Global earthquakes, tsunamis, and one gigantic hole through the planet.
  - Axial tilt altered to ~0.8° → **no real seasons** anymore.
  - Atmosphere behavior around the bore is bizarre (voiding / pressure changes).
  - Localized zones of extreme structural stress and weird gravity/pressure near the bore line.

- Politically:
  - Crown becomes both **ground zero** and **holy/strategic site**.  
  - Regions interpret the event as punishment, accident, test, etc.

### 2.2 Soulpulse (02-Soulpulse-Core)

- Before the Piercing:
  - The universe already runs on **friction between the Four Principles** (see next section).
  - There’s a universal “field” of potential; living things are powered by it but can’t *grab* it.

- The Piercing does three key things:
  1. Scrapes **“Soulpulse dust”** from the projectile into the planet and atmosphere.
  2. Seeds that dust into **water, stone, soil, and living bodies**.
  3. Re-tunes Te’Oga so that the planetary field **couples to mortal nerves** via that dust.

- Soulpulse is:
  - Not a new energy; it’s **new access** to an old cosmic engine.
  - Observable as resonance in material + body, tied to emotion, intention, and Principle-bias.
  - Always, always has **bodily cost**. No free spells.

- System stages:
  - Low-level “shiver” effects → controlled structural compensation → combat/weaponization → **Fathom Break** (god-tier, suicidal-level output).
  - Cost scales from fatigue/tremor up to organ failure, neurological collapse, and death.

### 2.3 The Four Principles and the Gods (03-Divine-Principles)

- Principles:
  - **Wholeness** – cohesion, caretaking, integration.
  - **Form** – structure, precision, boundaries, systems.
  - **Purity** – isolation, clarity, removal of contamination.
  - **Entropy** – decay, ending, tearing down.

- Hidden cosmic truth:
  - The universe exists as long as these four **never fully resolve their argument**.
  - If one “wins” or they reconcile, existence collapses / empties / freezes.
  - Their incompatibility = **engine of reality**.

- Dual-state gods:
  - Each Principle has:
    - A **Divine Aspect** (cosmic-level).
    - A **Mortal Mask** (a human on Te’Oga with a career and life).
  - During the ancient war, the gods were forced into mortal masks to prevent open annihilation.

- Named pairs (locked canon):
  - **Form** – Corlexi (god) / Xilcore (mask).  
  - **Wholeness** – Salee / Leesa.  
  - **Purity** – Mobel / Blemo.  
  - **Entropy** – Eries / Seeri.  

### 2.4 Cosmology & Counter-Spark (04-Cosmology-Confluence)

- The universe:
  - Is effectively a **confluence reactor**: friction between four Principles radiates power.
  - Existence is powered by **their disagreement**.

- Counter-Spark:
  - A meta-entity whose goal is to **resolve** that friction (force a “win” condition).
  - If successful, reality stabilizes into silence/death.
  - In saga canon: **Counter-Spark = Auditor**, revealed near the very end.

- Te’Oga’s role:
  - The Piercing + Soulpulse + Fathom Break = reopening an ancient conflict.
  - Jhace’s later Fathom Break is a **cosmic-scale event** that shifts this war.
