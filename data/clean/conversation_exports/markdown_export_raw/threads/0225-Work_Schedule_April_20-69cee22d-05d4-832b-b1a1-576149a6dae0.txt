[ASSISTANT]
**Hypoglycemia** = low blood sugar (typically under ~70 mg/dL).

**Common symptoms:**
- Shaky, sweaty, cold
- Fast heartbeat
- Dizzy or lightheaded
- Blurred vision
- Irritable, confused, or “out of it”
- In bad cases: fainting or seizures

**Fast fix (don’t overthink it):**
- Take **15–20g of quick sugar**:
  - Juice (½ cup)
  - Regular soda (not diet)
  - Glucose tablets
  - Honey or sugar
- Wait ~15 minutes, check how you feel, repeat if needed
- Then eat something with carbs + protein (sandwich, crackers + peanut butter) so it doesn’t crash again

**Causes:**
- Skipping meals
- Too much insulin/diabetes meds
- Heavy physical work without eating (your kind of job makes this common)
- Alcohol without food

If this is happening often, or you’re blacking out / nearly blacking out, that’s not “tough it out” territory—get it checked.
