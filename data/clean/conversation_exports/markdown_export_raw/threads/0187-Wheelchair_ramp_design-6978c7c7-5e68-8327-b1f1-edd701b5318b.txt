- **Top landing:** **36" deep minimum**, **60" preferred**, **level** (0–2% slope max).

### Overall footprint

- **Top landing:** 36" long × same width (if space allows).

**Option A (fast + strong): single framed ramp section**
- **Stringers/side beams:** (2) **2×8** treated, 60" long
- **Joists:** **2×6 treated @ 12" on center** (or 16" OC if you overbuild decking)

- Decking thickness: ~**1"**
- Joist thickness: **5.5"**

**Simplest method:** set the **finished deck height at top = +6"** relative to bottom surface. Then build the frame to match that, instead of trying to “calculate” from lumber thickness.
