What does it mean to grow on someone?

[ASSISTANT]
“To grow on someone” = you **didn’t like them (or liked them only a little) at first, but over time you start liking them more and more.**

It can apply to:
- **People:** “I didn’t like him at first, but he’s growing on me.”  
- **Things (music, food, shows, etc.):** “This song was mid at first, but it’s starting to grow on me.”

Core idea:
- First impression: **meh / unsure / maybe even annoyed**
- After spending time with them/it: **you notice good qualities**, you feel more comfortable, and your feelings shift toward **fondness or affection**.

It does **not** usually mean “I’m suddenly obsessed.” It’s more like a **slow burn** type of liking.
