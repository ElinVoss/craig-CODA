This is a placeholder corpus for future local pretraining experiments.

The text in this file is intentionally small and generic. It exists to define the expected plain-text format before any real corpus work begins.

A future corpus can be assembled from local sources, cleaned consistently, and tracked with clear provenance notes. For now, the goal is to keep the format simple and easy to validate.

The repository should remain readable on a CPU-first Windows machine. Large text collections, checkpoints, and exports belong on the D drive so they stay easy to find and manage.
